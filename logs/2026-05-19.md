## What we did
Fixed a persistent full-page gray overlay on mobile that had been blocking the site on every iPhone load. Diagnosed and tuned the two cup pop-out scroll animations. Made several sizing and positioning tweaks to the pop-out images.

## Decisions made
- **Gray overlay root cause found**: A single CSS line — `.nav-overlay { display: block; }` inside `@media (max-width: 900px)` — was making the full-screen nav backdrop (rgba 0,0,0,0.6) always visible on mobile. Removed that line. Every previous fix attempt (isolation, translateZ, color-scheme, #t=0.001, Reduce Motion) was targeting the wrong cause entirely.
- **Poster fade removed**: Removed the `<img class="hero-poster-img">` element and its JS/CSS logic. Video now plays directly with no fade-in transition.
- **Pop-out timing delayed**: Shifted cup scroll triggers from 0.30–0.55 → 0.55–0.68 so they animate while the section is fully in view, not sneaking in at the bottom corner.
- **Pop-out easing improved**: Replaced linear interpolation with smoothstep `t²(3-2t)` and tightened ramp window from 0.25 → 0.13 for a quicker, smoother feel.
- **Left blue cup**: Sized up 10% (550px → 605px → 666px), moved down 30px (bottom: -80px → -110px).
- **Right red cup**: Sized up incrementally (414px → 455px → 491px).
