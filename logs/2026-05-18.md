## What we did

Overhauled the typography system through a multi-step font exploration — tried Extenda, Big Shoulders Display, and Passion One before landing on Bungee (display) + DM Sans (body) as the final pairing. Redesigned the six event cards from 6 narrow columns into a 3×2 grid at 9:16 aspect ratio. Added a hover reveal effect where the overlay and text fade out, exposing the full background photo.

## Decisions made

- **Bungee** (Google Fonts) for all display/headings: modeled after venue marquee signage, chunky without being wide, has real character for a bar brand. Adam's stated preference was chunky/bold over thin/condensed.
- **DM Sans** (locally hosted variable font) for all body text, labels, small copy: Passion One was illegible at small sizes; DM Sans is highly readable at any size and pairs cleanly with Bungee.
- **Extenda rejected**: 80-Peta variant too wide ("fat"), 40-Hecto better but still not the right fit.
- **Big Shoulders Display rejected**: too thin/tall for the energy Harper's needs.
- **Passion One rejected as sole font**: great for display but unreadable at 0.6–0.8rem label sizes.
- **Event cards: 3×2 grid at 9:16**: previous 6-column layout was too narrow; full-page attempt was too large. 9:16 shows the full flyer photo composition. Container stays at 1200px max-width.
- **Hover reveal**: on hover, `.ecard-overlay` and `.ecard-inner` both fade to `opacity: 0` over 0.5s. Background photo zooms to scale(1.07) simultaneously. Clean, no JS needed.

## Current font stack

- `--font-display: 'Bungee', sans-serif` — loaded via Google Fonts link in index.html (requires internet connection to render)
- `--font-body: 'DM Sans', sans-serif` — locally hosted at Font/DM_Sans/DMSans-VariableFont_opsz,wght.ttf (weight range 100–900)

## Event cards current state

- Grid: `repeat(3, 1fr)`, gap 12px, inside `.thisweek-inner` max-width 1200px
- Aspect ratio: `9/16`
- Hover: overlay + text fade out (opacity 0, 0.5s), bg photo scales to 1.07
- Text sizes: ecard-name `clamp(1.8rem, 2.8vw, 2.6rem)`, ecard-day `0.85rem`, ecard-deals `0.88rem`
- Six cards: Monday (Karaoke), Tuesday (Honky Tonk), Wednesday (Half-Off), Thursday (Patio DJ), Friday (Club Rush), Saturday (Game Day → After Dark)
- Each card has its own color identity pulled from matching promo flyer photos
