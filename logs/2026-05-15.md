## What we did

Rebuilt the entire Harper's color system to be fully cohesive with the sun logo (yellow, red, navy, teal, cream). Then made the site feel alive by adding 8 SVG wave dividers between every section color-change, converting all buttons to pill shape, adding card radius and hover lifts, dot texture on cream sections, and counter block accents. Finally ran a performance pass: hero video changed from full preload to metadata-only, 14 images across the page gained loading="lazy", and all 5 videos now have poster frames so nothing shows black while loading.

## Decisions made

- **Dark background → deep navy (`#09081C`)**: The logo has no pure black. All dark sections now connect to the logo's navy family. Nightlife section, body background, and food/merch card backs all use navy-dark instead of `#111111`.
- **Red updated to `#E03020`**: Warmer and more orange, closer to the logo's vivid hand-drawn red.
- **Nav scrolls to navy, not teal**: More premium feel; teal stays as an accent color, not a background on interaction.
- **Legacy alias variables removed**: `--green`, `--green-deep`, `--amber`, `--amber-light` were misleading holdovers — all removed.
- **Pill buttons (100px radius)**: The logo's wave curls and hand-drawn aesthetic called for pills, not rectangles.
- **Wave dividers (SVG)**: 8 waves between sections — alternating arch and S-curve patterns — replace every hard horizontal cut. Added as plain HTML divs with inline SVG, no JS needed.
- **Dot texture on cream**: `radial-gradient` repeating pattern at 5.5% opacity on all cream sections. Adds depth without noise.
- **Merch grid fixed**: Was `1fr 1fr` (broken with 3 cards). Now `repeat(3, 1fr)` with `aspect-ratio: 3/4` on each card.
- **Hero video preload**: Changed `preload="auto"` to `preload="metadata"` — single biggest performance win. Browser no longer front-loads the entire video file before painting.
- **Poster frames on all 5 videos**: Hero uses `Harps_Feb11-28_no-logo_16x9.png`. Promo videos use thematically relevant photos. No more black boxes.
