Built for Every
Kind of Night.

Harper's has been East Lansing's gathering spot for twenty-eight years. Upstairs is the bar, the kitchen, the screens, and the patio. Downstairs is Club Rush fog cannons, concert lighting, and the loudest weekend on Albert Ave. Same family running the place since day one. Different scene every night of the week.

The Patio

Meet Me
on the Patio.

String lights, fire pits, and the best patio DJ in East Lansing.
Dinner crowd by seven. Loudest patio in town by eleven.

The patio is the heart of Harper's open-air seating, heaters when it's cold, and a DJ booth that turns the courtyard into the loudest open-air bar in East Lansing. Come for dinner under the string lights. Stay through last call. Either way, you'll see everyone you know.

Game Day

The Loudest Saturday
in East Lansing.

Every screen on the game. Doors at noon for tailgating, kitchen open through the fourth quarter, and the patio packed by sundown. Whether you're here for MSU football or watching the away game with 499 of your closest fans Saturday starts at Harper's.

On-Site Brewery

Brewed Behind
the Bar.

East Lansing's only working brewery. Brewmaster Kirk Kowalski runs six taps out of copper tanks you can see from your barstool pale ales, wheats, IPAs, stouts, and gluten-reduced brews that test below 10 ppm. The tanks behind the bar aren't decoration. They're the bar.
